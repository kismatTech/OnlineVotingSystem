  • How secure is the eVote Shield system?

    eVote Shield employs robust encryption and authentication measures, ensuring a secure and tamper-resistant online voting environment.

  • Can I trust the anonymity of my vote in the online system?

    Yes, eVote Shield maintains strict confidentiality standards, ensuring each vote is anonymous and protected throughout the entire process.

  • Can I change my vote after submitting it online?

    Once a vote is submitted, the system ensures its integrity. Changes are not allowed to maintain the sanctity of the voting process.

    Dedicated support is available to address technical issues promptly. Additionally, contingency plans are in place to ensure a smooth voting experience.
